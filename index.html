Xiangmin Fan (cv) is an Associate Professor in HCI Lab at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is also an Adjunct Associate Professor at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. Xiangmin is supported by CAS 100 Talents Program (中科院百人计划). His primary research direction is Human-Computer Interaction. Xiangmin's research interests include Intelligent User Interfaces, Mobile Interfaces, Human Computation, and their applications in Education and Healthcare. Xiangmin received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Pittsburgh (Ph.D. Advisor: Dr. Jingtao Wang), Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Shandong University.
